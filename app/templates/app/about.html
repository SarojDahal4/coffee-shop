About Us

Serving Since 2023

Our Story

Freshly graduated and facing a tough job market, I started a small coffee and tea shop called Fresho Coffee.

With my savings, family and friends support and a bit of luck, it quickly became a beloved community hub, offering not just drinks, but a warm, welcoming space for everyone.

Our Vision

At Fresho Coffee, our vision is to create a welcoming sanctuary where every cup of coffee or tea brings people together. We strive to foster a sense of community, inspire connections, and provide a cozy space where everyone feels at home. Through quality, sustainability, and warmth, we aim to be the heart of our neighborhood, brewing more than just beverages but also unforgettable moments and lasting relationships.

Quality and Sustainability: We are committed to sourcing the finest, ethically produced coffee beans and organic teas to ensure every cup is exceptional while supporting sustainable practices.
Community Engagement: By book readings and art showcases, we aim to cultivate a vibrant, inclusive community space where everyone can connect and share their passions.
Welcoming Atmosphere: We prioritize creating a cozy, inviting environment with comfortable seating, local art, and friendly service, making Fresho's a home away from home for our customers.
